About Us

As people, we must fly for a variety of reasons for survival, for work, and for pleasure.

Travel is a human right, not a luxury, in our view.

With the advent of technology, we assume that by making travel more available to all, we can make it more enjoyable for all.

Our goal is to fill vacant seats in cars by renting it and to make it possible for people to ride together.
